Steering Controller

Local tractor steering access

Before continuing

  • Turn on the FarmVista Steering Controller.
  • Open your phone’s Bluetooth settings.
  • Connect to the FarmVista Steering Controller.
  • Verify Bluetooth shows connected before proceeding.
  • Make sure the steering system and surrounding area are clear before operating.
⚠️

Bluetooth must be connected first

Opening the steering controller without first establishing the Bluetooth connection may cause communication errors or prevent the steering system from operating correctly.
