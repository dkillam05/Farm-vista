Equipment Dashboard

The Equipment module keeps your machinery organized—from tractors and sprayers to trucks and trailers. View, track, and maintain every asset in one place with details on service history, attachments, and performance notes.

Use the sidebar to navigate to Tractors, Sprayers, Combines, Implements, and other categories. Record maintenance, assign equipment to operators, and manage uptime across the farm season.

Overview

  • 🚜  Maintain records for tractors, sprayers, and combines
  • 🧰  Log maintenance dates, hours, and service notes
  • 📋  Assign equipment to farms, fields, or operators
  • ⚙️  Track attachments, implements, and configurations
  • 📅  Plan seasonal usage and track downtime
