Reports Dashboard

The Reports module is your command center for analysis, insight, and documentation. It brings together field results, AI-generated summaries, and custom performance reports—all formatted consistently for internal and external use.

From here, you can generate AI Reports for agronomic trials, view Predefined Templates like harvest summaries, or browse your Report History. Each report is timestamped, branded, and exportable as a polished PDF.

Available Report Types

  • 🛠️  AI Reports (Custom) — Generate custom analyses using AI tools and input data
  • 📚  Predefined Reports — Use preset templates for harvest, inputs, and yield trials
  • 🗂️  AI Report History — Access, download, and compare past generated reports

Features

  • 📅  Automatically timestamps and organizes reports by season
  • 🧾  Exports cleanly to PDF with Dowson Farms branding
  • 🔍  Integrates with crop production data for accurate analysis
  • 🧠  Supports AI-generated summaries and insights
  • ☁️  Stored securely and accessible across all devices
