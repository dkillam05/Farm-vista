Grain Dashboard

The Grain module centralizes your on-farm and delivered grain: bins, bags, contracts, and tickets. Track inventory in real time, reconcile loads against contracts, and keep a clean record of grades, moisture, and shrink across the season.

Use the sidebar to jump into Grain Bin Inventory, Grain Bag Inventory, Grain Contracts, and Grain Tickets (OCR). Log movements, verify settlement details, and stay on top of what’s stored, what’s hauled, and what’s sold.

Overview

  • 🛢️  Track on-farm inventory by bin and bag
  • 📄  Manage contracts and monitor remaining balances
  • 🎟️  Capture tickets (incl. OCR) with grades and dockage
  • 📊  See moisture, test weight, and shrink history
  • 🔁  Reconcile loads from field → storage → delivery
