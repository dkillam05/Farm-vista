How the model works

Storage is the model’s internal water “tank” (0..Smax).
Each day: storage += infilMult*rain − (dryPwr * dryMult)
DryPwr comes from temp + solar + wind − humidity (normalized 0..1).
Wetness Index is storage/Smax × 100.

Soil Wetness slider biases starting storage.
Drainage slider changes how much rain sticks + how fast it dries.
