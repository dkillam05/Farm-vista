Teams & Partners

The Teams & Partners area keeps your people network organized—employees, sub-contractors, vendors, and key partners. It’s the human side of the operation, built for quick access to contact info, work roles, rates, and documentation.

Use this section to update employee records, onboard new vendors, store W-9 and insurance forms, or keep contractor agreements in one place. It’s also where you can define roles and responsibilities for everyone who helps make the season run smoothly.

Overview

  • 🧑🏼‍🌾  Manage employee details, contact info, and positions
  • 🧰  Track sub-contractor services, agreements, and rates
  • 🏪  Maintain vendor lists for parts, supplies, and inputs
  • 📖  Keep a farm dictionary of shared terms and references
  • 📁  Attach W-9, COI, and other compliance documents
