Expenses Dashboard

The Expenses module helps employees and managers record small purchases and reimbursements made on behalf of the farm. Whether a purchase was made using a personal card or a company credit card, this area keeps all receipts and details organized in one central place.

Each entry can include a receipt photo, vendor name, category, and amount. Submissions can be reviewed and approved for reimbursement or logged for month-end credit card reconciliation. Use the sidebar to start a new reimbursement, view pending reports, or browse past submissions.

Overview

  • 💳  Record purchases made on personal or company credit cards
  • 🧾  Attach photos of receipts for office verification
  • 🏷️  Categorize expenses by type, vendor, or project
  • 📅  Compile monthly reimbursement reports for review
  • ✅  Track approval status and payment completion
